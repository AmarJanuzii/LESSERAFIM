LE SSERAFIM

A 5-member Korean Pop / R&B girl group made up of SAKURA, CHAEWON, YUNJIN, KAZUHA, EUNCHAE.

With their first mini album ‘Fearless’ they were the second artists to debut from SOURCE MUSIC, a global entertainment production company with a mission to produce artists with a defined world view, creating a culture of bold creativity.
In October of the same year, they released their second mini album 'ANTIFRAGILE'.

The group are 'LE SSERAFIM', which is an anagram for 'IM FEARLESS'.
With their fresh, inventive music and performance, LE SSERAFIM aims to empower young people from all over the world - from all walks of life.
