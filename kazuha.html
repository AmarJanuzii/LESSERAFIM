I am
KAZUHA

Hello there! My name is Kazuha, and I'm a multi-talented artist from Japan. I'm signed under Source Music, where I'm the stunning visual and also a rapper, singer, and ballerina for the girl group LE SSERAFIM..

However, in August 2021, I joined Source Music, and on April 9, 2022, I was confirmed as the sixth and final member of LE SSERAFIM. We made our debut on May 2 with our first mini album, FEARLESS, and it was an incredible experience.

Before making my K-pop debut, I had quite the background in ballet. I've danced at some of the world's top ballet institutions, including the Bolshoi Ballet Academy in Moscow, the Royal Ballet School in London, and the Hashimoto Sachiyo Ballet School & Jr in my homeland of Japan. In 2020, I even enrolled at the Dutch National Ballet Academy in Amsterdam. It was there that I caught the eye of the Source Music CEO in mid-December 2021.

Finally, after much anticipation, I was announced as the fifth member of LE SSERAFIM by Source Music on April 8, 2022. We went on to make our highly-anticipated debut on May 2 with the release of our first mini-album, FEARLESS.
